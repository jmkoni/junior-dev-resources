Want to get a job as a web developer?

This site contains a direct path that you can take to be successful!

How to Use This Site

  1. Determine which language you want to learn.
  2. Determine your learning style. Do you learn best by video? Tutorial? MOOC? Different people learn differently, so find the resource that works best for you.
  3. Join a Slack group for new coders. We recommend Code Newbie. They have groups for every language, so no matter what you pick you will have support.
  4. Look for beginner friendly groups in your area. Meetup usually has a lot of these. If you are a member of an underrepresented group in tech, there might even be a group that caters directly to you (ex: PyLadies and Trans*H4CK).
  5. After you have the basics down, make sure to take a look at the general knowledge page. This page contains information on topics that are crucial to life as a full-time developer.
  6. Contact us if you have any questions or suggestions!
